# Psalms


# Week 1 - Worship God Wholeheartedly
June 21, 2026

- To worship God with a whole heart, remember all that God has done for you and your life
- Worship is like a thermometer, and a thermostat. It tells if you are grateful of God in your life

### 01 - Remember what God has done

- Don't take health for granted. Thank God that you can see, hear, talk, sing, move
- When God takes away something, God will give you something better
- In everything, give thanks. Even the bad because God will work on you through it

### 02 - Recognize who God is

- "I want to know You. Show me who You are. Show me Your glory"
- We have a loving Father
- Who can go to the president while he is sleeping to tell that he is thirsty? His child. In the same way, we are God's children. We can go to Him anytime
- Without discipline, your character will not grow

### 03 - Rest in God's Sovereignty

- God rules over all of us and everything under the heavens. No one can say "What have you done?" to Him. Give this authority to anyone else, and it is very dangerous. But we can have full security because we know that God is faithful, good, and loving to His children always
