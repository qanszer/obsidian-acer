# Green Flags Before You Sign

- Clear role & expectations
- Salary is upfront, walang pa-safe
- Contract = same as usapan
- Work hours & OT rules are clear
- Maayos at mabilis kausap
- Nakilala mo actual manager/team
- May growth, hindi lang puro tasks
- Benefits explained, hindi "madami" lang
- Clear if WFH, hybrid, or onsite
- You felt heard, not rushed
- No pressure to decide agad
- Culture feels real, not scripted
