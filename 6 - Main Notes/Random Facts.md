**Dental health quality** is directly proportional to the **consistent, proper intake of fluoride** and the **minimal consumption of sugary foods**.

While other factors like genetics and professional care play a role, these two factors represent the most significant, controllable, and proven methods for protecting tooth enamel and reducing the incidence of cavities.
