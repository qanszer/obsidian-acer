# 01 - God's Way to Heaven


**Water analogy about God's Holiness and how He can't compromise**
- If you are thirsty and I offered you a drink of water, will you drink it?
- Now if before I offered it to you, I dropped a speck of dust from my shoe into the drink and you saw it -- will you still drink it?
- It is the same with God. He can't compromise His holiness even if we are almost perfect in our lives. We still fall short from the glory of God.


**Usual Objections**
1. "But I have tried to live a good life" - you cannot save your life through good works; it is only by His grace
2. "But I have not done anything scandalous like adultery or murder" - 


Why is the resurrection essential to the Christian faith?
- Because it validates who Jesus claimed to be -- Son of God and Messiah
