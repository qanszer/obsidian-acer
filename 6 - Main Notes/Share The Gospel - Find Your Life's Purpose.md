# Find Your Life's Purpose

Hi, do you know that every person is looking for a purpose and not a lot of people actually find it?

Some look for it in money, in career, in having so many Facebook friends, while some in relationships. Not saying these things are wrong; in fact, these things can give us a sense of purpose, but it's never going to be enough. 

You know the reason why most people don't find that true purpose? It's because they're looking for it at the wrong places. We can only find that purpose in God. 

In fact, God's purpose for our lives is to have a personal and genuine relationship with Him, but we have a big problem and that is sin. 

Sin separates us from God; that's why our greatest need is a Savior. We need God, and God loves us so much that He sent His one and only Son, Jesus, to die for your sins and mine. And He's inviting us to have a genuine relationship with Him and have and find that true purpose.

So if that's your desire today, I want to lead you in a simple prayer. Can we all bow down our heads? If that is you and God is speaking to you, you pray this prayer with me. You say: "Jesus, I realize that I need a Savior. I'm a sinner and I need a purpose in life. Only in You can I find that purpose. So Jesus, I ask for Your forgiveness. I confess that I need You desperately and with all my heart I receive You today as my Lord and Savior. Come into my life, starting today. I will follow You all the days of my life. In Jesus' name I pray." 

If you just prayed that prayer, or if you have any other questions, please do contact us. God bless you.
