# Personal Engagement Log

Record all the notable conversations you've had for the day.

Every Saturday, after dinner, devote yourself to a process of self-examination, review, and appraisal.

Skim the logs for the past week, and ask yourself these questions:

 - '‘What mistakes did I make that time?’'
 - '‘What did I do that was right—and in what way could I have improved my performance?’'
 - '‘What lessons can I learn from that experience?’'

This will make you unhappy, sometimes very unhappy. But it will improve your ability to make decisions. And it will aid you in all your contacts with people.
