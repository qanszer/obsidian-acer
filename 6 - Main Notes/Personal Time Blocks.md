# Daily Time Blocks

---

## Evening Before
**(10:00 PM - 11:00 PM)**


### Wind Down Routine
**10:00 PM**

- Plan out tomorrow's tasks
- Soap face wash, cleanser + moisturizer,  toothbrush
- Set alarm
- Pray


### Lights Out
**11:00 PM**

- Sleep


---

## Morning
**(6:30 AM - 9:00 AM)**


### Slow Start
**6:30 AM**

Start the day at your own pace. If you wake before the alarm, get up—fragmented sleep won't help.

- 10-minute walk outside
- bath + skincare
- cook rice if none on ref
- do house chores
- eat breakfast
- open time: bebetime, reading, catching up on to-do's, or social time with friends


---

## Work Block
**(9:00 AM - 3:30 PM)**


### Pre-Work Transition
**9:00 AM**

**Purpose:** Shift into "developer mode" mentally and physically

- Change into work outfit (signals role shift)
- Brief family: communicate boundaries + check-in windows
- **Synthetic commute:** walk + work-related podcast, return "to the office"


### Deep Work Begins
9:30 AM

- Clear desk surface
- All notifications off, Do Not Disturb on
- Noise-cancelling headphones active


### Lunch Break
**12:00 - 1:00 PM**

- Eat, recharge, stretch


### Deep Work Continues
**1:00 - 3:30 PM**


### Work Ends
**3:30 PM**


---

## Evening
**(3:30 PM - 11:00 PM)**


### Transition Out
**3:30 PM**

- **Synthetic commute:** 10-15 minute walk to mentally process the day
- Change into workout clothes (role shift: "employee" → "person")
- Exercise


### Open Time
**5:00 PM**

**Default options:**
-  Read a book
-  Entertainment
-  Social time
-  Household/Personal to-do's
-  Dinner preparation


### Scheduled Bebetime
**8:00 PM**


### Wind Down Routine Begins
**10:00 PM**


---

## Weekend
**Saturday - Sunday**

**No work block**—structure is flexible for rest and personal activities
