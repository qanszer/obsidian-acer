# Daily Time Blocks

---

## Evening Before
**(10:00 PM - 11:00 PM)**


### Wind Down Routine
**10:00 PM**

- Plan out tomorrow's tasks
- Journal
- Soap face wash, cleanser + moisturizer,  toothbrush
- Set alarm
- Pray


### Lights Out
**11:00 PM**

- Sleep


---

## Morning
**(6:30 AM - 9:00 AM)**


### Slow Start
**6:30 AM**

Start the day at your own pace. If you wake before the alarm, get up—fragmented sleep won't help.

- cook rice if none on ref
- drink barley
- 10-minute walk outside
- bath + skincare
- do house chores
- eat breakfast
- open time options: 
	- bebetime
	- reading
	- catching up on to-do's
	- social time with friends


---

## Work Block
**(9:30 AM - 3:30 PM)**


### Deep Work Begins
9:30 AM

- Clear desk surface
- All notifications off, Do Not Disturb on
- Noise-cancelling headphones active


### Lunch Break
**12:00 - 1:00 PM**


### Afternoon Nap
**1:00 - 1:30 PM**


### Deep Work Continues
**1:30 - 4:00 PM**


### Work Ends
**4:00 PM**


---

## Afternoon to Evening
**(4:00 PM - 11:00 PM)**

### Workout
**4:00 PM**


### Open Time
**5:00 PM**

**Default options:**
-  Read a book
-  Entertainment
-  Social time
-  Household/Personal to-do's
-  Dinner preparation


### Scheduled Bebetime
**8:00 PM**


### Wind Down Routine Begins
**10:00 PM**


---

## Weekend
**Saturday - Sunday**

**No work block**—structure is flexible for rest and personal activities
