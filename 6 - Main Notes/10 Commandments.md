# Week 1 - Ten Commandments
April 19, 2026


### Testimony Learnings

- "If we were to face Jesus one day, would He ask what religious organization you belonged to, or if you truly knew Him and had a personal relationship with Him?"
- Some people only know Jesus in their head, but did not really know Him in their heart. They hide behind their religious upbringing and practices that make them feel good and secure. They are open to God, but only on their own terms.
- Transformation to people's lives take time. It can take more than a year. The point is that it is a gradual process.
- Following Jesus is not about maintaining practices, but about knowing Him and worshiping Him according to His truth.


**Purpose of the 10 Commandments**

1. To guide, protect and bless us
2. To reveal our sinfulness (like a mirror)
3. To point us to Jesus Christ
4. To reveal God's character and desire
5. To teach how we are to worship and please Him


- "I love you so much that I am giving you this law so that you can live your life without regrets"
- The moral framework of western civilization is affected by the 10 commandments

> 
> *What if you could lose everything in your life---except one thing---what would you choose to keep?*
> 
> Whatever you don't want to lose... is what you truly worship.
> 

- Because of reputation, money, or security, we don't care what happens to other people anymore. It's like we don't have eyes to see what's happening around us, or ears to hear the sufferings and struggles around us. That is not what God wants us to be.
- You sing "What a powerful name it is", but why do you not turn to Him when you need help?

### 01 - Worship God Exclusively

It's like how when you decide to marry one person, you choose to back away from the billion other people to marry. 

When God looks inside your heart, He should not see any other thing besides Him. Money, house, bank account, a person, or any other thing.

People usually don't reject God---they add to Him.

### 02 - Worship God Rightly

Don't pray to God with conditions. Dont pray to him, "Lord if you do this for me in my life, I will believe you and always pray to you from now on"

**F.I.R.S.T.**
If God is first, He is first in your:
- Finances - how you give and trust
- Interests - what captures your attention
- Relationships - who shapes your decisions
- Schedule - what you prioritize
- Trouble - where you run in crisis

People, since they love God so much, they make carvings for Him to see Him. But God says in Exodus 20:1-6, no image can imitate Him. He does not want carvings for Him.

> 
> *"Our hearts are restless until they rest in You."*
> 

**Preaching Notes**

- Use funny, expressive facial expressions to keep the audience engaged. Don't be afraid to make yourself look stupid doing it.
- Ask the audience if they understood what you just said
