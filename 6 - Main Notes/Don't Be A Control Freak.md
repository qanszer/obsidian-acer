# Don't Be A Control Freak

*"Life happens. Life, happens. No matter what, life is unpredictable to a certain degree. So instead of trying to control all these things---thinking that you have to accummulate a list of things first before having or doing a particular thing---just live life and trust that you can adapt to whatever happens.*"

**-- Uncle Giovanni (High Integrity Skills)**
