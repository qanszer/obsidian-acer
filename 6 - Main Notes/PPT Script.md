### Ideation Methods

For our ideation stage, we chose two methods: brainstorming and mind mapping. We chose brainstorming because it allowed every member of our team to freely contribute ideas without judgment, ensuring that no potential solution was overlooked regardless of how big or small the idea was. Mind mapping was chosen as a complementary method because it helped us visually organize and connect those ideas into meaningful clusters, making it easier to see patterns and relationships between features.


**BRAINSTORMING**

The group gathered and shared different ideas related to the G-MIL Builder website redesign.

Members suggested features, layout improvements, content additions, and user experience enhancements. Ideas included improving navigation, adding project galleries, and enhancing visual design.


**MINDMAPPING**

We grouped similar ideas and structured them into categories using a mind map. This helped identify the strongest and most practical concepts. 

From there, we analyzed which ideas best addressed the user needs and project goals, leading to the selection of the final concept.

### Final Idea

**Our final idea is a website designed to eliminate the most critical barriers clients face when hiring contractors namely — distrust, pricing transparency, and lack of guidance.** 

The website will feature:
- transparent pricing tools (cost calculators, package comparisons, and timeline simulators), 
- a verified portfolio with before-and-after visuals, 
- and multiple communication contact methods and a quick inquiry form — directly addressing the pain points of our user personas. 

To build credibility, it will showcase licenses, client testimonials, and a verified project portfolio, while also offering a step-by-step guidance section and an "Avoid Mistakes" resource for first-time renovators. 

Post-project support will be integrated through a digital document vault and a maintenance support page, ensuring long-term client relationships as needed by personas. 

Ultimately, the design strategy is anchored on four pillars — **Transparency, Trust, Convenience, and User-Centeredness** — to make the entire home construction journey, from planning to after-sales, seamless and stress-free.
