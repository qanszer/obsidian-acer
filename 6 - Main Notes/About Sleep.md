# About Sleep

The Philippines has been consistently ranked as the **most sleep-deprived country in Southeast Asia** and within the top four globally.

## 01 - The Importance of Sleep

Balanced diet, exercise, and sleep. Those are the three pillars of health, and sleep is actually the most important between the three.

#### Consequences to continuous lack of sleep

1. **Physical Health**:
	- Increases risk of cardiovascular diseases, stroke, and Alzheimer's
	- Damages your immune system
	- Disrupts blood sugar levels to the point that you would be classified as pre-diabetic
2. **Mental Health**:
	- Contributes to major psychiatric conditions like depression, anxiety, and suicidality
3. **Diet and Weight Loss**:
	- Makes you feel hungry despite being full--a proven recipe for weight gain
	- If you attempt to diet while lacking sleep, most of the weight you'll lose will come from lean body mass, not body fat

## 02 - How to Sleep Quickly

1. Do your best to **sleep and wake up at the same time everyday**, even on weekends. Your body will naturally start feeling sleepy at bedtime because you trained your circadian rhythm properly
2. **Cool room** = faster sleep. Ideal is at 18-19°C but this is not strict
3. **Darker room** = faster sleep. Either have blackout curtains or use a sleeping mask
4. Personal advice not backed by studies:
	- When actually attempting to sleep, **inhale deeply and exhale** as if you're sinking into the bed
	- If you tend to sleep with your mouth open, **tilt your head downward** toward your shoulder
	- **Drop down your shoulders** (toward your stomach for those confused)
	- **Think about what you'll be doing** first thing in the morning after waking up
5. **Don't drink caffeine 8 hours before sleeping** (~2pm). Caffeinated drinks include coffee, green/black tea, soda, and energy drinks.
6. If possible, **don't use devices 1 hour before sleeping**. If not possible, **use the night mode feature** of your device to filter the blue light in your screen

#### If still struggling to sleep

1. If you're not asleep after 20 minutes, **get up**. Do something boring in dim light and only return to bed when sleepy
2. **Turn the clock away from view** because checking the time creates anxiety, which keeps you awake

#### Honorary mentions for sleeping quickly

- No meals/snacks 2-3 hours before bed. Digestion interferes with sleep
- Don't use your phone in bed. Your bed should only be associated for sleeping. 
- No alcohol 3-4 hours before bed
- Create a wind-down ritual that trains your brain to think that sleep is coming
- Exercise improves sleep quality. Avoid working out 2-3 hours before bed
