# My Commonplace Book

A collection of my favorite quotes. Quotes that struck something in me.

---


*"You can't control reality if you can't control yourself."*


*"Make awkward steps forward, than cowardly steps backward."*


"*You don't make choices. The choices make you.*"


*"Remove the word 'try' from your vocabulary. Decide if you will do or will not do something."*


*"Fast success builds ego. Slow success builds character."*


*"When a man has no purpose in life, he distracts himself with pleasure."*


*"Idleness is the devil's playground."*


*"Make the important things in life seem unimportant, if you want to succeed in your endeavors."*


*"A great idea is like a match stick. It needs to be lit up in order for it to produce fire."*


*"All people struggle. But not all people pity themselves."*


*"Without courage, wisdom bears no fruit."*


*"Whatever it is that stirs your soul, listen to that. Everything else is just noise."*


*"You can do hard things. Uncertainty is the nature of life, but self-trust is the antidote."*


*"There is something so surprising about how far you had come, simply by showing up everyday."*


*"Life happens. Life, happens. No matter what, life is unpredictable to a certain degree. So instead of trying to control all these things---thinking that you have to accummulate a list of things first before having or doing a particular thing---just live life and trust that you can adapt to whatever happens.*"  - Uncle Gio


"*We're always changing anyway, so why not change for the better? Why not try to improve your life no matter what happens?*"  - Uncle Gio


*"It is the individual who is not interested in his fellow men who has the greatest difficulties in life and provides the greatest injury to others. It is from among such individuals that all human failures spring."*  - Alfred Adler


*"Be hearty in your approbation and lavish in your praise, and people will cherish your words and repeat them over a lifetime—repeat them years after you have forgotten them."*  - Dale Carnegie


*"What you missed was not created for you and what was created for you will not be missed, so be assured."*


*"Always look at the beautiful things in every situation."*
