# Eye Witness - Lessons From People Who Saw Jesus Suffer


# Week 1 - 
March 22, 2026
**Luke 22**


**Lesson from Herod Antipas**
There are people who seek Jesus, but only because they want something from Him

Reflection: Do you follow Jesus because you want to be free from poverty? Or anything along those lines?


Many people in the Bible were direct eye witnesses to Jesus and His miracles, but in spite of that, they had negative responses to it:

| Eyewitness         | Response           |
| ------------------ | ------------------ |
| Pilate             | Pleasing people    |
| Sanhedrin          | Rejected the truth |
| Herod              | Miracle seeker     |
| Barrabas           | Prefers violence   |
| Peter              | Self protection    |
| Women of Jerusalem | Prefers religion   |

**This means that it really is not up to us in changing the hearts of other people to have faith in Jesus. Our only purpose is to share it--God will do the rest.**
