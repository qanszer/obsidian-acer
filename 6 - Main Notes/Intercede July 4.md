# Breakthroughs Happen When We Follow God

**LISTEN**
- Restoration begins when we are humble enough to admit that we are broken and need a restore
- Naaman is the captain of a King's army. But he was also a leper. He did not let his disability define him
- We choose restoration by having the humility to listen, for the voice of God can come from any person or place

**OBEY**
- Sometimes we expect God to move in a certain way to solve our problems. That it requires great works or dramatic witnessing. When all we need to do is obey Him
- Breakthrough and restoration happens in God's time, God's way, and God's purpose
