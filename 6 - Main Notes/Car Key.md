# Car Key

A son was about to graduate, and he asked his dad for a car as his present. His dad agreed, and so the son was overjoyed. 

The day came, and as he went home and expected a car key as his gift, his dad instead gave him a Bible. He was furious. His parents had been trying to make him get to know God for the longest time now, but this moment became his bottling point. So furious and angry, he left home and lived by himself for 15 years. From that day on, he never made contact with his father.

One day, his mother called him, saying, "Your father fell ill. He's on his deathbed and is about to die. Don't you want to see him for the last time?". Not wanting to have any regrets, he went.
