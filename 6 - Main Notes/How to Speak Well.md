# How to Speak Well

The goal in speaking well is not how deep your vocabulary is. The goal is to transfer your thoughts from your head to theirs without losing anything in between.


## 01 - The Grandma Rule

- Talk as if you're talking to a grandma
- Don't use complex words. Explain it as simply as possible
- If a grandma can understand it, then everyone can

## 02 - The "Kinda Like" Rule

- Use the word to connect the unfamiliar with the familiar
- Connect complex subjects with easy-to-understand examples. This makes it easy for others to understand confusing subjects

## 03 - The One Sentence Rule

- If you can't explain what you're trying to do or say in one sentence, then you don't understand the subject well enough yet. Not because it is too complex, but because you haven't run it properly through your own brain to simplify it
- Try these:
	- What is a mentor?
	- What is discipline?
