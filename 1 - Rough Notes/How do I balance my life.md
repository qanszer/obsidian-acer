Things to Grow

1. Wisdom 

     - application of knowledge

     - God wants us to have wisdom

     - 1 whole book dedicated to wisdom (proverbs)

     - wisdom is better than rubies and other things

     - How to gain wisdom? 

         a. read the word of God - the most powerful wisdom you can receive is not through conferences, but through personal quiet time in reading His Word

         b. surround yourself with the right people - two types of people (Godly people and ungodly people)

         c. prayer

         d. be sensitive to the leading of the Holy Spirit

2. Stature

      - your physical body matters

      - we are God's temple---and the Holy Spirit dwells in us

      - things that destroy our body include:

          a. sexual sin - flee from it like joseph

          b. overworking - rest

          c. undisciplined lifestyle

3-4. Favor with God and favor with man

      - people usually rely too much on human connection than with God

      - how to gain the favor of God: obey Him and His commandments

      - if your SQ is high, your IQ and EQ will follow

      - to be spiritually mature:

           a. be filled with the Holy Spirit

           b. abide in Jesus and follow the Lord
