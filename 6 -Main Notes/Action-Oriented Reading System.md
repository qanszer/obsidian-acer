### **Simple 3-Part System:**

**1. While Reading:**

-  Mark passages that resonate or seem useful
-  Note critical questions
-  Note personally specific "I should try this" moments


**2. After Each Chapter:** Write in Obsidian:

-  **Main Idea:** What's this chapter about? (1-2 sentences)
-  **Key Takeaways:** 3-5 bullet points of useful concepts
-  **Actions:** What will I actually DO with this? (Specific, concrete actions)


**3. After Finishing the Book:**

-  **Book Summary:** One paragraph overview
-  **Top 3 Insights:** What were the most valuable things?
-  **Implementation Plan:** What am I committing to apply? When? How?
